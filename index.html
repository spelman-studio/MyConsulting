About Me

Jane Spelman is a Freelance Website Designer for Spelman Studio, a Marketing Consultant for Delaware Area Career Center, and works in Communications for the Miami University College of Creative Arts and Sciences with certifications in Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and Premiere. She also has skills in software such as Canva, Buffer, Trello, and much more. She completed the Digital Design program at Delaware Area Career Center and graduated from Olentangy Berlin High School. They are attending Miami University for its Emerging Technology in Business and Design major. She is passionate about expressing creativity while excelling in the technological world and leading teams to success through strong communication skills and project organization.
